Team Askalon presents Smash Mania 2025 at Cadets de Bretagne. There's singles, 3 vs 3 and Ladder competitions for Ultimate along with several other fighting/platform fighters. Somnio Noctem

The Somnio team is scoping up to be bring The Netherlands a new 🕸spooky🕸 Major, Somnio Noctem hosted in the H20 Esports Campus in Purmerend (near Amsterdam). There's singles, doubles and 3 vs 3 crew competitions for Melee and Ultimate along with several other games to compete in and exciting (Halloween themed) side events.
